# 🧠 MediScope AI

AI-driven health platform that transforms medical reports into clear decisions, actionable guidance, and doctor connections.

---

## 🚀 Overview

MediScope AI is a smart healthcare platform designed to help users understand their medical reports and take the right next step.

Instead of just showing numbers, the system:

* explains results clearly
* connects them with lifestyle factors
* provides safe, personalized guidance
* and enables direct doctor consultation

---

## ❗ Problem

Medical reports are often:

* hard to understand for non-medical users
* full of complex terms
* disconnected from daily habits
* lacking clear next steps

This leads to:

* confusion
* unnecessary anxiety
* or ignoring important signals

---

## 💡 Solution

MediScope AI transforms raw medical data into:

* 🧠 **Clear Decisions**
* 📊 **Personalized Insights**
* ⚡ **Immediate Safe Actions**
* 👨‍⚕️ **Doctor Connection Options**

---

## 🔥 Key Features

### 🧾 Medical Report Analysis

* Upload lab reports (images)
* AI extracts and interprets data using Google Gemini

### 🧠 Decision Support System

* Concern Level (Low / Moderate / Higher)
* Clear explanation of what it means
* Personalized reasoning based on real values

### ⚡ Lifestyle Integration

* Sleep, stress, and activity analysis
* Correlation with clinical findings

### 🔍 Possible Contributors

* Identifies factors that may influence results
* Based on lifestyle + report data
* Clearly marked as non-diagnostic

### 🎯 Actionable Guidance

* What you can do now (safe actions)
* Condition-based (not generic)

### 👨‍⚕️ Doctor Platform

* Doctors can create and publish profiles
* Patients can:

  * search by specialty
  * view doctor listings
  * book appointments (clinic / video)

### 🌍 Multilingual Support

* English 🇬🇧
* Türkçe 🇹🇷
* العربية 🇸🇦 (with full RTL support)

---

## 👤 User Flows

### 🧑‍⚕️ Patient

1. Sign in as Patient
2. Choose:

   * Upload report
   * or Contact a doctor
3. View AI-generated results
4. Take action or book consultation

---

### 👨‍⚕️ Doctor

1. Sign in as Doctor
2. Create a public profile:

   * name
   * specialty
   * clinic location
   * consultation price
   * availability
3. Publish profile
4. Receive bookings

---
